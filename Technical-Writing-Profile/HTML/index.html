What is Technical Writing?

Technical writing in the context of public health involves the creation of documents, reports, manuals, grant proposals, and guidelines. In general, technical writers simplify, arrange, and document complex public health information. We often appeal to healthcare professionals, policy makers, researchers, and the general public. If you have any questions about technical writing, feel free to contact me!
